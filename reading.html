Currently Reading: Where The Library Hides

Sequel to What The River Knows, Where The Library Hides follows Inez Olivera as she must decide her fate following her cousin’s murder, her mother’s betrayal, and her uncle’s ultimatum. Tío Ricardo has given Inez few options to acquire her inheritance, leaving her seemingly with one option: marrying his former employee Whitford Hayes. The question is, can she trust him? Set in 1885 and split between Egypt and Buenos Aires, Where The Library Hides is fantastical historical fiction that beautifully concludes the Secrets of the Nile duology.

Book Club Book: Ink Blood Sister Scribe

Esther and Joanna’s family have guarded rare and ancient magical books for generations. However, magic has created a divide between the two sisters. Joanna is left to guard the fortune with her father while Esther tries to escape her mother’s fate. After having been separated for a decade, the sisters must reunite after their father’s mysterious death while reading a book Joanna has never seen before. On a mission to preserve their magical legacy, the sisters discover that the books have a far deeper history than they realized.

Last Read: What the River Knows

Inez Olivera is a member of the high society of nineteenth century Buenos Aires. Inez has everything she could want, except for her nomadic parents who frequently leave her behind for Egypt. When Inez is notified of her parents’ death, she inherits their fortune and becomes the dependent of her archeologist uncle. Inez decides to sail to Cairo to investigate her parents’ deaths and is instantly connected to the everpresent magic of the old world. As she is led down a mysterious path, Inez is constantly at odds with her uncle’s infuriating (and handsome) assistant. Will she find out what really happened to her parents or risk arriving at the same fate?
